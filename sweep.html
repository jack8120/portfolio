Sweep

CRUD App

While completing the MERN Full Stack Bootcamp with Tech Educators I worked part time with a friends chimney sweeping business. Convinced that many time consuming tasks could be streamlined with technology I began researching how to create an appp that could manage much of the admin work load.

Many of these tasks could take considerably less time with the use of an effective customer database. A CRUD app seemed to cover a lot of the features I wanted to implement.

Having set up a React, Express / Node and MongoDB architecture I am working on developing the specific functionality required by the business I am working with. As a work in progress the current test state of the app can be visited above.
